About Solve India

Solve India is an AI-powered civic innovation platform that empowers citizens, students, innovators, NGOs, and government organizations to work together to solve India’s real-world challenges. Instead of depending on other countries for solutions, Solve India harnesses the creativity and talent of India’s young minds to build innovative, homegrown solutions for a stronger, smarter, and self-reliant nation.

Our Vision

To make India a global leader in innovation by transforming every challenge into an opportunity through technology, collaboration, and citizen participation.

Our Mission

* Empower citizens to report public issues.
* Encourage students and innovators to develop practical solutions.
* Connect NGOs, volunteers, and government departments.
* Use AI to prioritize and recommend solutions.
* Build a transparent, collaborative platform for national progress.

How It Works

1. Report a problem with details, photos, videos, and location.
2. AI categorizes the issue and identifies duplicate reports.
3. Citizens, students, and experts submit innovative solutions.
4. Community members vote for the best ideas.
5. NGOs and government agencies review and adopt solutions.
6. Track the progress until the issue is resolved.
